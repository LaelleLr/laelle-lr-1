Our roots

      

The Designer club has always been something I’ve wanted to build. It’s been a dream since I was a little girl. The Designer club was created in 2019. The name came to us when we were having breakfast at All time in Los Feliz, LA.       

All of our pieces are ethically produced and manufactured in Portugal. We have designed each piece to feel timeless and to work together. We believe in timeless designs that will work for years to come.
